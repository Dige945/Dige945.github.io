Music

一些我常常会回去听的音乐人和专辑。A small shelf of records I keep returning to.

陶喆 · David Tao

陶喆 David Tao

1997 · Mandopop / R&B · 侠客唱片 / Shok Records

中文：大家常说的“蓝专”。它把 R&B 的节奏、和声和华语旋律揉在一起，听起来一点也不老；《飞机场的10:30》《沙滩》《爱，很简单》到现在还是很能把人拉回某个蓝色夜晚。

English:A debut that made Mandopop feel newly elastic: bedroom-soul intimacy, polished R&B instincts, and songs that still carry the quiet shock of a first arrival.

推荐 / Picks: 飞机场的10:30 / Airport in 10:30, 沙滩 / Blue Moon, 爱，很简单 / I Love You

方大同 · Khalil Fong

橙月 Orange Moon

2008 · Mandopop / R&B · 华纳唱片 / Warner Music

中文：温暖、克制，又有很漂亮的律动感。方大同把中文 R&B 做得松弛而细腻，这张专辑像一盏偏橙色的小灯，适合夜里慢慢听。

English:A mellow, romantic Khalil Fong record: understated grooves, soft-lit melodies, and the kind of songwriting that sounds casual until it quietly stays with you.

推荐 / Picks: Singalongsong, 小小虫, 三人游, 每个人都会

方大同 · Khalil Fong

梦想家 The Dreamer

2024 · Mandopop / R&B · Fu Music

中文：这张听起来更轻，也更珍贵。很多声音像是在有限身体条件里慢慢雕出来的，复古、自然、简约，但仍然有方大同那种明亮的音乐想象力。

English:Made after years of illness and interruption, The Dreamer feels like a modest room with the windows open: compact, soulful, and full of gentle resolve.

推荐 / Picks: 才二十三, Tango, 那沙漠里的水, 没啥好说

张震岳 · Ayal Komod

跟著感覺走 Go With The Flow

2025 · Pop / Rock / Singer-songwriter · 滚石唱片 / Rock Records

中文：很久之后再回来的阿岳，听起来还是那个把生活放在音乐前面的人。这张专辑有一点粗粝，也有一点海边的风，像是边走边把心事说完。

English:A loose, lived-in return after a long gap: plainspoken rock songs, weathered tenderness, and an easygoing belief that the walk matters as much as the destination.

推荐 / Picks: 浪人的..., 梅雨季, 跟著感覺走, 答案

卢广仲 · Crowd Lu

励志论 healism

2021 · Mandopop · 添翼创越工作室 / Team Ear Music

中文：它不是鸡血式的励志，更像一种“也没关系”的自我修复。卢广仲把怪、暖、孤独和幽默放在同一张桌上，越听越觉得轻轻地被接住。

English:As its English title suggests, healism is less a pep talk than a healing practice: oddball pop colors, gentle absurdity, and Crowd Lu's bright voice finding room for complicated feelings.

推荐 / Picks: 励志论 / C'MON, 雨时多云偶阵晴 / uWEATHER, 狂迪 / Crazy Disco, 轻轻 / iRipple

Daft Punk

Random Access Memories

2013 · Disco / Funk / Electronic · Columbia Records

中文：它不像一张只为了舞池存在的电子专辑，更像 Daft Punk 写给七八十年代录音室、现场乐手和流行音乐黄金时代的一封情书。精致到近乎奢侈，但很会发光。

English:A lavish studio album built around live players, disco memory, and machine-made romance; it turns electronic music toward human touch without losing the duo's chrome-plated mystery.

推荐 / Picks: Give Life Back to Music, Giorgio by Moroder, Get Lucky, Instant Crush
