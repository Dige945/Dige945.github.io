陶喆 · David Tao

陶喆 David Tao

1997 · Mandopop / R&B · 侠客唱片 / Shok Records

Works from the end of the last century can still be appreciated today.

常说的“蓝专”，上世纪末的作品放在现在依然能打。

推荐 / Picks: 飞机场的10:30 / Airport in 10:30, 沙滩 / Blue Moon, 爱，很简单 / I Love You

方大同 · Khalil Fong

橙月 Orange Moon

2008 · Mandopop / R&B · 华纳唱片 / Warner Music

A mellow, romantic Khalil Fong record: understated grooves, soft-lit melodies, and the kind of songwriting that sounds casual until it quietly stays with you.

温暖、克制，又有很漂亮的律动感。方大同把中文 R&B 做得松弛而细腻，这张专辑像一盏偏橙色的小灯，适合夜里慢慢听。

推荐 / Picks: Singalongsong, 小小虫, 三人游, 每个人都会

方大同 · Khalil Fong

梦想家 The Dreamer

2024 · Mandopop / R&B · Fu Music

This one sounds lighter and more precious. It's Khalil's last album, and I will always cherish the memory of this soul boy.

这张听起来更轻，也更珍贵，是大同最后一张专辑，永远怀念这位soul boy。

推荐 / Picks: 才二十三, Tango, 那沙漠里的水, 没啥好说

张震岳 · Ayal Komod

跟著感覺走 Go With The Flow

2025 · Pop / Rock / Singer-songwriter · 滚石唱片 / Rock Records

A loose, lived-in return after a long gap: plainspoken rock songs, weathered tenderness, and an easygoing belief that the walk matters as much as the destination.

很久之后再回来的阿岳，听起来还是那个把生活放在音乐前面的人。这张专辑有一点粗粝，也有一点海边的风，像是边走边把心事说完。

推荐 / Picks: 浪人的..., 梅雨季, 跟著感覺走, 答案

卢广仲 · Crowd Lu

励志论 healism

2021 · Mandopop · 添翼创越工作室 / Team Ear Music

The wound has been struck ten thousand times! I'll practice replenishing blood another ten thousand times!.

伤口被打击一万次！就再练补血一万次！

推荐 / Picks: 励志论 / C'MON, 雨时多云偶阵晴 / uWEATHER, 狂迪 / Crazy Disco, 轻轻 / iRipple

Daft Punk

Random Access Memories

2013 · Disco / Funk / Electronic · Columbia Records

A lavish studio album built around live players, disco memory, and machine-made romance; it turns electronic music toward human touch without losing the duo's chrome-plated mystery.

它不像一张只为了舞池存在的电子专辑，更像 Daft Punk 写给七八十年代录音室、现场乐手和流行音乐黄金时代的一封情书。

推荐 / Picks: Give Life Back to Music, Giorgio by Moroder, Get Lucky, Instant Crush
